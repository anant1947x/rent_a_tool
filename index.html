Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our tool rental service.

To rent a tool, you need to be a registered student with a valid student ID. You'll also need to create an account on our platform, provide a valid form of payment, and sign our rental agreement. For certain high-value tools, a security deposit may be required.

Standard rental periods range from 1 to 7 days for basic rentals. Semester Pass members can rent for up to 14 days, and Group Plan members for up to 21 days. Extensions are possible if the tool isn't reserved by another student. Late returns incur additional fees.

We understand that accidents happen. Minor wear and tear is expected and covered. For significant damage, you may be charged a repair fee or replacement cost. We offer an optional damage protection plan for Rs-999 per rental that limits your liability to Rs-1999 regardless of the damage.

Yes! We offer basic usage instructions for all tools at pickup. For more complex equipment, we provide detailed manuals and online video tutorials. We also offer in-person training sessions: Rs--500 for basic rentals, Rs-299 for Semester Pass members, and free for Group Plan members.

We recommend reserving tools in advance, especially for popular items or during busy periods like end-of-semester project time. Basic rentals can reserve up to 1 week in advance, Semester Pass members up to 2 weeks, and Group Plan members up to 3 weeks.

Our main pickup location is in the Engineering Building, Room 103. We're open Monday-Friday from 9am-6pm and Saturdays from 10am-4pm. During finals week, we extend our hours to 8am-8pm on weekdays to better accommodate student projects.
