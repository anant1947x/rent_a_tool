Rental Charges

Our rental system is designed to be affordable for students while ensuring proper maintenance of tools.

Normal Tools

Screwdrivers, Multimeters, Soldering Irons, etc.

  • Rental Rate: 0.5% to 1% of the tool's original price per 3 days
  • Billing Cycle: Rental charges are calculated in blocks of 3 days (Example: 3 days, 6 days, 9 days, and so on)
  • Example: A ₹3,000 multimeter would cost ₹15-30 for a 3-day rental
Browse Normal Tools

Expensive & Sensitive Tools

3D Printers, DSLR Cameras, CNC Machines, etc.

  • Rental Rate: 1% to 2% of the tool's original price per 3 days
  • Billing Cycle: Same as above — in multiples of 3 days
  • Example: A ₹30,000 DSLR camera would cost ₹300-600 for a 3-day rental

Late Return Fine

If a user returns a tool late, a fine will be applied.

Fine Amount:

Double the normal 3-day rental charge will be added for each 3-day delay.

Example: If normal rent for 3 days is ₹30, late fine = ₹60 for each late 3-day block.

Credit System

Compensation for unavailable pre-booked tools.

How it works:

  • The affected user receives a Credit Token
  • The credit provides a free rental worth 3 days for their next booking
  • This credit is funded from a part of the late fine collected from the user who caused the delay

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can rent tools from Rent-A-Tool?

Only verified college users (students, teachers, or employees) with valid college ID can rent tools from our platform. This ensures that tools remain within the college community.

How long can I rent a tool?

Tools can be rented for periods that are multiples of 3 days (3, 6, 9, 12 days, etc.). The maximum rental period is typically 15 days, but this may vary depending on the tool's demand and availability.

What happens if I return a tool early?

Early submission of tools doesn't lead to a refund. The rental charge is calculated based on the booking period, not the actual usage period.

What if the tool I need is not available?

You can join the waitlist for that specific tool. When the tool becomes available, you'll receive a notification. If your pre-booked tool isn't available on time due to a late return by another user, you'll receive a credit token as compensation.

What if I damage a tool?

If a tool is damaged during your rental period, the admin will assess the damage. Depending on the severity, you may be charged a repair fee or the full replacement cost of the tool. Minor wear and tear from normal use is expected and won't incur charges.
